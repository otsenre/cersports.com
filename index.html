Under the hood

HOW THE MODEL WORKS

Three independent engines run every morning. One consensus output. No opinions.

01
Data Scrape
Live game logs, rest days, back-to-backs, team ratings, injury context, and historical matchup data pulled fresh every morning via Playwright.
02
Rule Engine + ELO
A rule-based model scores every matchup on net rating, pace, fatigue index, and form. An ELO + XGBoost model trained on a decade of data produces win probability and predicted margin.
03
Consensus + Publish
All three outputs cross-referenced. Agreement scoring produces a unified CCR rating. Only plays above threshold qualify. Report published automatically with social cards by 8:05 AM.
Our rating system

WHAT IS CCR?

The CERSports Confidence Rating is a 0–100 score produced by our consensus model. It measures how much edge the system has detected across all three models combined.

90+
Elite
Full model agreement. Rare. Maximum edge detected.
80–89
Strong
Standard qualifier for posted picks. Strong consensus.
68–79
Solid
Solid data lean. Below our posting threshold.
<68
Noise
We don't post what the model doesn't believe in.
