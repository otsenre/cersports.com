How it actually works

Three steps. That's the whole machine.

1

Six models vote

Every morning, six independent models crunch the numbers on the day's matchups — team form, injuries, history, even what the odds are saying — and each one weighs in. Think of it as a committee, not one loud opinion.

2

They agree on the best calls

Where the models line up, that's a pick — a prediction about one game. Each pick gets a confidence rating, so you always know how strongly the data backs it. The single most-confident one is posted free, every day.

3

Every result gets posted

When the games finish, we grade each pick and post the outcome in public — the wins and the losses. Nothing gets quietly deleted. The running record is right there for anyone to check.
